# Recruitment run 
Recruitment Run is a dynamic Android arcade game that simulates the journey of a recruit on their way to the induction center. The game combines fast-paced gameplay with advanced Android features to create an immersive experience.

# Game Features
Military Recruitment Theme Experience the journey to the induction center in this immersive arcade-style runner inspired by the Israeli recruitment process.

Dual Control Modes Choose your playstyle: use the device's Accelerometer for tilt-based steering or switch to classic On-Screen Buttons for precision movement.

Dynamic Obstacle Logic Dodge randomized military-themed obstacles across a 5-lane road that tests your reaction time and agility.

Location-Based High Scores Every record is saved with your real-world coordinates using Google Maps API, showing exactly where you achieved your top score.

Immersive Audio & Haptics High-quality "Crash" sound effects powered by SoundPool and haptic vibration feedback on every collision for a realistic feel.

Persistent Leaderboard Your top 10 scores are saved locally using SharedPreferences, ensuring your legacy is preserved even after closing the app.

Speed Difficulty Scaling Challenge yourself with different speed levels (Slow/Fast) to master the road to the recruiting office.
